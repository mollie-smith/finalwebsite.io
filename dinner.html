4-Ingredient Ravioli Lasagna

This easy lasagna is one of our favorite meals! It is great for busy weeknights or easy entertaining! Amazingly cheesy, creamy, comforting ravioli made in 30 minutes or less, perfect for those busy weeknights! It’s all about balance, no? That’s where this creamy, comforting, cheesy ravioli comes in. And for those of you thinking you can’t break your diet for this, well, let’s just say that your diet can always start again next week.

Prep Time: 10 Mins Cook Time: 20 Mins Total Time: 30 Mins

Recipe Measurements:

Ingredients Amount
Marinara Sauce 2 Jars
Frozen Ravioli 2 Packages
Mozzerella 1 1/2 Cups
Parmesan Cheese 1/2 cup
Basil 1/3 cup chopped

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F.
  2. Spread half of a jar of marinara sauce evenly across the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Add 1 package of ravioli in an even layer over the sauce. Sprinkle ¾ cup of mozzarella cheese and ¼ cup of Parmesan cheese over the sauce.
  3. Bake the lasagna for 30 minutes. Uncover and bake for an additional 15 minutes. Remove pan from oven let sit for 5 minutes. .
  4. Enjoy!
